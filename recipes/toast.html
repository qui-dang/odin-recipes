Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Eggs
  • Butter or Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Directions

  1. Heat up butter or oil on frying pan
  2. Crack eggs into frying pan
  3. While eggs are cooking toast your bread in the toaster
  4. Once toast and eggs are cooked, salt and pepper the eggs
  5. Enjoy!
